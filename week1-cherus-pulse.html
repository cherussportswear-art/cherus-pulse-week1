Match of the Week

UOE Pippers 33–32 Daystar Falcons

A classic Nyayo thriller — the Pippers squeaked past Daystar in a match defined by clinical finishing and late-game drama. Cliff Titto (17) and Alfam Ogwora (16) lit up the court.

  • Nyayo crowd impact: atmosphere lifted player intensity after halftime.
